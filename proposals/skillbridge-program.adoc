* Python
* Salt/Jinja
* C

Functional knowledge in one or more languages/key areas specified is a requirement for applicants. Service member's active contributions are pushed to upstream open-source projects that power the Georgia Cyber Range. The contributions will become a permanent fixture in the respective project(s).
The work of service members, if accepted upstream, will be pulled down and improve the suite of capabilities offered by the Georgia Cyber Range.

=== Executive Director Shadow Program
The Executive Director Shadow Program is specifically designed to provide senior service members with executive leadership experience in a complex and dynamic organization outside of their normal military experience.  The unique ecosystem of the Georgia Cyber Center provides senior service members with an opportunity to interact directly and routinely with local, state, and federal government, academia, and private sector entities that are focused on cyber operations and cybersecurity.  In addition to gaining exposure to senior leaders in both the public and private sector, service members will gain specific experience in Business Development, Program Management, Grants Management, Innovation Management, Organizational Strategy, and Organizational Leadership.

*Positions Available:*

*   Program Manager
*   Business Development Managers
*   Grants Manager
*   Senior Strategist
*   Director of Innovation
*   Chief of Staff

=== AU Cybersecurity Operations Center 

In October 2020, the new AU Cybersecurity Operations Center (CSOC) will open in the Georgia Cyber Center (GCC).  The mission of the CSOC is to protect both the Augusta University and Augusta University Health System networks and systems, providing monitoring, security solutions, incident response, threat hunting, and Governance/Risk/Compliance (GRC) services.  Plans include providing SOC-as-a-Service to outside entities as the CSOC services mature.  Additionally, to ensure that Augusta University faculty and students can utilize innovative technology to improve the learning experience both onsite and at a distance, the AU Division of Information Technology operates an Audio/Visual System Engineering team that designs, installs, and commissions state of the art technology enhancements in classrooms and meeting spaces per ANSI/AVIXA standards.

*Positions Available:*

*   SOC (Cyber Defense) Analyst
*   Cyber Security Engineers
*   GRC Analysts
*   GRC Engineers
*   Audiovisual Systems Engineer
*   Audiovisual Technician/Installers
*   Director of Audiovisual and Event Services

The beginner track is designed for service members with no prior training or experience in the IT and cybersecurity field. 
Completion of the beginner track provides service members with the knowledge to obtain the following top industry certifications:

// Upon completion, service members will have all the knowledge to obtain the following certifications:

    _Industry recognized certification that provides a global benchmark for best practices in IT network and operational security._
